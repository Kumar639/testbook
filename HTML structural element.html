Opening ceremony of Ram mandir

The Ram Mandir ceremony will begin on January 16, 2024 and end with the Pran Pratishtha ceremony on January 22, 2024. The Pran Pratishtha ceremony will be held between 12:15 PM and 12:45 PM. The ceremony will be attended by Prime Minister Narendra Modi and other dignitaries, including sportspersons. The guest list also includes industrialists like Mukesh Ambani, Gautam Adani, and Ratan Tata, and Bollywood icons like Amitabh Bachchan.

india vs afghanistan series T20 full result

India beat Afghanistan 3-0 in the 2024 T20I series. The series included three matches, with the third match ending in a double Super Over thriller. Both teams scored 212 runs in their 20 overs, and were also tied in the first Super Over. India won the second Super Over to clinch the series. Here are the scores from the three matches: 1st T20I: Afghanistan 158-5 (20), India 159-4 (17.3) 2nd T20I: Afghanistan 172 (20), India 173-4 (15.4) 3rd T20I: India 212-4 (20), Afghanistan 212/6 (20)
